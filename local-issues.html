Tourist Traffic Congestion

Galle experiences severe traffic congestion, especially during tourist season. The narrow roads around Galle Fort and coastal areas become bottlenecks during peak hours and weekends.

Tourist Waste Management

High tourist volumes create waste management challenges, particularly around the Fort area, beaches, and popular restaurants.

Power Infrastructure

Aging electrical infrastructure struggles to meet increasing demand from tourism and residential growth.

Water Management

Seasonal water shortages and drainage issues during monsoon season affect both residents and businesses.
