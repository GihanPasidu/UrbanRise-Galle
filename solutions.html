Smart City Solutions

AI-Powered Traffic Light System

Pilot Project

Smart traffic lights that adjust timing based on real-time traffic flow data collected from cameras and sensors. This system can reduce congestion by up to 25% at busy intersections.

Reduces waiting time by 30%
Decreases fuel consumption
Pilot location: Colombo Fort

Smart Public Transport System

Implemented

A mobile application that provides real-time information about bus and train schedules, occupancy levels, and estimated arrival times. The system also enables cashless payments for seamless commuting.

50,000+ active users
Covers 200+ routes
Available in Colombo, Kandy, and Galle

IoT Parking Management

Planning Stage

Smart parking system using sensors to identify vacant spaces. Drivers can use a mobile app to find and reserve parking spots, reducing the time spent searching for parking and minimizing traffic caused by circling vehicles.

Target: 2,000 parking spaces
Reduces parking search time by 70%
Proposed for Colombo CBD

IoT-Enabled Waste Management

Implemented

Smart waste bins equipped with fill-level sensors that optimize collection routes. This system ensures bins are only emptied when needed, reducing collection trips by 30%.

Reduces collection vehicles by 15
Saves 25% on fuel costs
Deployed in Colombo Municipality

Smart Solar Street Lighting

Implemented

Solar-powered LED street lights with motion sensors that dim when no activity is detected and brighten when vehicles or pedestrians approach. This system reduces energy consumption by up to 60%.

100% solar powered
500 lights installed
Main arterial roads in Colombo

Urban Rainwater Harvesting Network

Pilot Project

Integrated system of rainwater collection at public buildings, with smart distribution for landscape irrigation and non-potable uses. The system includes IoT sensors to monitor water levels and quality.

Collects 70,000L per rainfall
Reduces municipal water use by 40%
Pilot at Colombo Municipal Council
