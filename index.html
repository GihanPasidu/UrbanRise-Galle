About UrbanRise Galle

UrbanRise Galle is dedicated to transforming our historic coastal city through innovative technology that respects our rich heritage. We address Galle's unique urban challenges while preserving the character that makes our city a UNESCO World Heritage site.

Our platform brings together heritage experts, urban planners, local businesses, and residents to collaborate on creating smarter, more efficient, and environmentally friendly solutions that honor our past while building our future.
